## 4. Post-Launch (30 days)

### Week 1
- Respond to every piece of feedback
- Fix critical bugs within 24 hours
- Collect 5+ testimonials
- Write "building in public" update

### Week 2-4
- Start content marketing cadence
- Begin outreach to potential users
- Set up referral mechanism
- Analyze which channels drove best users
