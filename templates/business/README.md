# Business Template

Document templates for product strategy, monetization, and go-to-market. Use with [solopreneur-skills](https://github.com/madeinhash/solopreneur-skills) business skills to generate these documents from a product idea.

## Templates

| Template | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `templates/brd.md` | Business Requirements Document — product overview, features, monetization, market analysis, GTM strategy, timeline |
| `templates/pricing-strategy.md` | Pricing strategy — tiers, free-to-paid conversion, competitive pricing, revenue projections |
| `templates/launch-plan.md` | Launch plan — pre-launch checklist, channel strategy, hour-by-hour launch day, first 100 users playbook |

## Usage

1. Use the `generate-brd` skill from [solopreneur-skills](https://github.com/madeinhash/solopreneur-skills) to auto-generate a BRD from your product idea
2. Or fill in the templates manually
